**Classification rules:**

- **broadcast**: p > 0.01 and p/(p+q) > 0.4 (innovation dominant)
- **peer**: q > 0.1 and q/(p+q) > 0.7 (imitation dominant)
- **mixed**: both p and q non-trivial
- **unfit**: no suitable segment or fit diverged
